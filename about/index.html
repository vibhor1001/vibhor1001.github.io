About PropertyFlow

Compliance-first short-term let management, built for the UK.

PropertyFlow is the all-in-one platform that replaces the entire STL tech stack — AI pricing, multi-channel distribution, guest messaging, compliance tracking and financials — in one dashboard. We help UK short-term let operators stay compliant and maximise revenue as regulation reshapes the market.

Our mission

The UK short-term let market is being reshaped by regulation. Mandatory licensing in Scotland, a registration scheme coming to England, and local visitor levies are filtering out non-compliant operators — and rewarding the professionals who do it properly.

We built PropertyFlow so operators of any size can run a fully compliant, fully optimised business from a single dashboard, without stitching together a channel manager, a pricing tool, a compliance tracker and an accounting package. One platform, three ways in: free to try, self-run for 5% of booking revenue, or a full 50/50 operational partnership.

What we stand for

Principles that run the platform

Compliance first

Being legal is a competitive advantage. Every property we touch runs on a clear compliance pathway — licences, planning and certificates tracked, never a gap.

Everything included

No add-ons, no hidden fees, no upsells to unlock core features. AI pricing, channels, messaging, compliance and financials come standard on every plan.

Start free, scale simply

Try the PMS free on one property. Run your whole portfolio for 5% of booking revenue. Or become a Partner on a true 50/50 split. No setup fees, no lock-in.

Operators in control

However hands-on you want to be, you keep ownership and the guest relationship. The platform, and the partnership, works for you — not the other way around.
