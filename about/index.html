Our Story

In October 2022, Edinburgh introduced mandatory short-term let (STL) licensing. Overnight, hundreds of landlords found themselves non-compliant with no clear path to legality. Planning permissions were impossible. Temporary licences expired. The licensing regime created chaos.

PropertyFlow was founded to navigate this complexity. We saw that Edinburgh's property owners needed a partner who understood the regulatory landscape deeply—not just a property manager.

Today, our portfolio operates at 100% STL compliance with zero council enforcement action. We've handled every scenario: planning refusals, LRB appeals, Para 1.22 cycling, licence renewals, and emergency evictions. We've learned what works.

Now we're scaling that expertise to help other Edinburgh landlords and investors reclaim control of their properties.

Our Approach

We're not property managers. We're compliance strategists. Here's how we think:

Zero-Gap Licensing

Your property operates legally 24/7. We don't wait for planning. We run temporary licence + full licence + planning permission in parallel. If planning is refused, we appeal via LRB or cycle via Para 1.22 Scottish Government guidance. No gaps. No ambiguity.

LRB Appeals & Planning Cycling

97% of Edinburgh STL planning applications are refused. We don't accept that. We handle LRB appeals (grounds for review) professionally. We understand Para 1.22 cycling—consecutive temporary licences while working planning. Most operators don't. We do.

Professional Vetting & Legal Defence

Every guest is screened: identity, address, payment method, previous host reviews, fraud checks. If anything goes wrong—damage, illegal activity, non-payment—we handle it. PropertyFlow holds £10M public liability, £5M employer's liability, and £100K professional indemnity per claim through AXA — covering our business operations. Landlords should maintain their own buildings and contents insurance.

AI-Powered Yield Optimization

Real-time market data (Airbnb, Booking, 50+ channels) feeds our AI pricing engine. We optimize rates daily based on demand, seasonality, competitor pricing, and council levy impact. You get maximum yield, legally.
