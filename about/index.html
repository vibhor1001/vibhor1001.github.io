Our Story

In October 2022, Edinburgh introduced mandatory short-term let (STL) licensing. Overnight, hundreds of landlords found themselves non-compliant with no clear path to legality. Planning permissions were impossible. Temporary licences expired. The licensing regime created chaos.

PropertyFlow was founded to navigate this complexity. We saw that Edinburgh's property owners needed a partner who understood the regulatory landscape deeply—not just a property manager.

Today, our portfolio operates at 100% STL compliance with zero council enforcement action. We've handled every scenario: planning challenges, licence renewals, and every compliance scenario. We've learned what works.

Now we're scaling that expertise to help other Edinburgh landlords and investors reclaim control of their properties.

Our Approach

We're not property managers. We're compliance strategists. Here's how we think:

Zero-Gap Licensing

Your property operates legally 24/7. We ensure continuous legal operation through our proprietary compliance approach — running applications in parallel so there are never gaps in your licence status. No gaps. No ambiguity.

Appeals & Planning Strategy

97% of Edinburgh STL planning applications are refused (STL Solutions, 2025). We don't accept that. We handle all appeals professionally and ensure continuous legal operation through our proprietary compliance approach. Most operators don't have a strategy for planning refusal. We do.
