How a Partner earns

A simple 20% of net revenue. Split fairly.

You charge landlords a management fee of 20% of each property's net revenue — what's left after direct running costs. That fee is shared 50/50: half to you for owning the client, half to PropertyFlow for running the operation and fronting the costs.

It's recurring income that compounds with every property you add. Here's an illustrative example of how a well-run two-bed can perform.
