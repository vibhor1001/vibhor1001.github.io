Marketing Manager

📍 Edinburgh / Remote UK ⏰ Full-time 💼 ESOP + Competitive Salary

We're looking for a Marketing Manager to drive PropertyFlow's growth from pilot launch to market leadership. You'll own our entire marketing strategy — from brand positioning and content creation to lead generation and community building. This is a unique opportunity to shape the marketing function from day one at a funded PropTech startup.

What you'll do

  • Develop and execute go-to-market strategy for our March 2026 pilot launch
  • Build PropertyFlow's brand presence across LinkedIn, industry publications, and events
  • Create compelling content: case studies, blog posts, email sequences, and landing pages
  • Drive lead generation through SEO, paid campaigns, and partnerships
  • Manage relationships with Edinburgh landlord associations and property networks
  • Track metrics and optimise campaigns based on data-driven insights

What we're looking for

  • 3+ years marketing experience, ideally in B2B SaaS or PropTech
  • Proven track record of driving growth at an early-stage startup
  • Strong writing skills — you can explain complex products simply
  • Experience with HubSpot, Google Analytics, and paid advertising platforms
  • Understanding of the Scottish property market is a plus
  • Self-starter mentality — you'll be building the marketing function from scratch
