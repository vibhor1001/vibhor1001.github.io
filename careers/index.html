About PropertyFlow

PropertyFlow Technologies Ltd was founded in December 2025 in Edinburgh. We're a funded PropTech startup building a cloud-based platform for short-term rental operators -- automating multi-channel distribution across major booking platforms like Airbnb and Booking.com, AI-powered dynamic pricing, and compliance tracking for UK STL regulations.

Alongside the platform, we offer property management as a service to landlords -- either as a corporate let or fully managed service. We're scaling rapidly, targeting Edinburgh's 4,700-property addressable market.

The team is small, the pipeline is growing fast, and we're looking for people who want to build something from the ground up.

Why Work Here

Early-Stage Impact

We're pre-launch and scaling fast. You'll build functions from scratch, not inherit someone else's playbook. Every team member shapes the company directly.

Compliance-First Culture

We do things properly. Edinburgh's STL licensing regime is complex, and we've built our entire approach around regulatory compliance. Rigour matters here.

Technology-Driven

Cognito pricing, automated compliance tracking, multi-channel distribution. We're a technology company that happens to work in property, not the other way around.

Genuine Flexibility

Edinburgh-based with real hybrid flexibility. The office is our base, but we trust our team to work where they're most productive. No rigid desk hours.

Autonomy & Ownership

We hire people we trust and let them run. Side projects and independent thinking are encouraged. You'll have real commercial exposure and direct impact on growth.

Marketing & HR Coordinator

Edinburgh (Hybrid) Full-time or Part-time Reports to Head of Commercial Growth

A hands-on marketing and commercial support role. You'll own PropertyFlow's day-to-day marketing -- from content and social media through to lead generation and CRM -- while also getting involved on the commercial side, speaking to landlords, handling objections, and helping convert interest into booked meetings.

Ideal for a recent graduate or someone with 1-2 years of experience who's already active on social media and has a feel for what makes content land. If you're running a side project, building a brand, or hustling on something of your own -- that's the mindset we're looking for.

Key Responsibilities

  • • Plan and write marketing content for landlords and STL managers across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Facebook. Own the content calendar, scheduling, publishing, and community engagement.
  • • Manage the relationship with external content creators end to end -- briefing, deadlines, reviewing deliverables, and ensuring output is on-brief and on-brand.
  • • Build and manage email campaigns and newsletters aimed at landlords. Post-launch, take ownership of paid advertising across social and search channels.
  • • Qualify inbound leads from social channels and convert them into booked meetings for the commercial team.
  • • Manage day-to-day HR processes using Sage -- onboarding, employee records, contracts, right-to-work checks, and compliance documentation.

What We're Looking For

  • ✓ Strong written communicator who can create content that resonates with a B2B audience
  • ✓ Active on social media with real understanding of how platforms work for growth
  • ✓ Comfortable context-switching between creative marketing and commercial support tasks
  • ✓ Genuinely interested in property, PropTech, or the short-let market

Property Portfolio Manager (Senior)

Edinburgh (On-site) Full-time, Permanent Competitive salary Reports to CEO

A senior project management role leading PropertyFlow's expansion into direct property acquisition while driving SaaS platform growth. You will manage a portfolio of commercial projects including property acquisitions, renovation programmes, compliance cycles, and platform onboarding initiatives. This is not a traditional estate agency or lettings management position.

Start date: September 2026. 37.5 hours per week, Monday to Friday. Skilled Worker visa sponsorship available for the right candidate.

Key Responsibilities

  • • Lead the property acquisition pipeline -- identify, evaluate, and project-manage the purchase and development of residential units for conversion into licensed short-term lets.
  • • Conduct site assessments using civil engineering / building surveying expertise, evaluating structural condition, renovation requirements, and cost estimation.
  • • Manage the compliance calendar across all properties -- STL licence renewals, gas safety certificates (CP12), EICR reports, EPC ratings, PAT testing, fire safety, and legionella risk assessments.
  • • Drive SaaS platform growth by onboarding new property owners from Edinburgh's 4,700-property addressable market.
  • • Coordinate a team of 6 across operations, commercial, technology, and on-the-ground property management. Prepare portfolio-wide performance reports and strategic recommendations for the CEO and board.

Essential Requirements

  • ✓ Degree-level qualification (RQF Level 6+) in civil engineering, construction management, building surveying, or business management
  • ✓ 10+ years project management experience managing multi-stakeholder programmes with budgets, timelines, and deliverables
  • ✓ Technical knowledge of building assessment: structural integrity, renovation scoping, cost estimation, and building safety regulations
  • ✓ Experience with regulatory compliance in property or construction (safety certificates, licensing, planning permissions)
  • ✓ Financial management skills: P&L oversight, acquisition business cases, portfolio performance reporting

Desirable

  • + MBA or equivalent postgraduate business qualification
  • + Experience with short-term rental, hospitality, or property technology platforms
  • + Knowledge of Scottish STL licensing regulations and Edinburgh's Control Area requirements
  • + Experience managing large-scale government or institutionally funded projects
