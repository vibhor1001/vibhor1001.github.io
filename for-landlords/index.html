Frequently Asked Questions

What OTA channels does PropertyFlow support? ▼

PropertyFlow connects to Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Expedia, Agoda, Trip.com, Google Vacation Rentals, and more — all managed from one dashboard. Rates, availability, and bookings sync in real time across every channel. No more logging into 5 different extranets.

How does the AI pricing engine work? ▼

Our pricing engine analyses demand, local events, competitor rates, seasonality, and day-of-week patterns to suggest optimal nightly rates. You review and approve suggestions from the calendar view, or enable auto-apply to let the system adjust rates automatically. Most hosts see a 15–30% revenue increase.

How much does PropertyFlow cost? ▼

PropertyFlow pricing is one continuous model — no separate plans to choose. Your first 3 properties are always free. From property #4, it's just £9.99 per extra per month. Every OTA channel (Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Expedia, Agoda, Trip.com, Hopper, Google Vacation Rentals, Direct) is available from day one — no upgrade required. Airbnb bookings are always 0% commission. Non-Airbnb OTA bookings are 5% per booking while you're on your first 3 free properties, dropping to 3% from your 4th property. No card required to sign up, no setup fees, no lock-in.

Is there a lock-in contract? ▼

No. PropertyFlow has no long-term contracts or lock-in periods. You can add or remove properties at any time, and you stay in full control of your listings, pricing, and strategy.

Can I manage my cleaning team through PropertyFlow? ▼

Yes. PropertyFlow includes a built-in team management module. Assign cleaners to properties, auto-create cleaning jobs from bookings, track clock-in/out times, manage checklists with photo verification, and handle scheduling — all from the same platform.

How does the unified inbox work? ▼

Every guest message from every OTA arrives in one inbox. Reply to Airbnb, Booking.com, and VRBO guests from the same screen. Use message templates for quick replies, set up auto-responses for check-in instructions, and never miss a message again.
