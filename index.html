Licensing is strict, and it's different in every city.

Short-term let rules change from one council to the next, and they keep moving. Which licence you need, when it renews, whether the property requires planning permission, which safety certificates must stay current. Get it wrong and you risk fines, or being unable to let at all.

Licence applications

Knowing exactly which licences a property needs in its city, and getting each application right the first time.

Renewals & deadlines

Every licence and certificate expires. Each renewal date is mapped and acted on, so renewals aren't left to chance.

Planning permission

More areas now require planning consent to let short-term. We flag where it applies and handle the submission.

Safety certificates

Gas, electrical, fire and EPC certificates kept current, with plenty of warning long before they are due.

Frequently asked questions

How does PropertyFlow pricing work now?

There are three ways to work with us. The Free plan gives you the full platform on up to 3 properties — free, with Airbnb at 0% commission and other OTAs at 5%. Premium starts from your 4th property at just £9.99 per extra property per month, and drops other-OTA commission to 3%. Become a Partner is a true 50/50 partnership: on top of the platform, we run every ground operation like linen, cleaning, changeovers and logistics, plus all backend operations, so you can focus on guests and growth.

What is included in the Free plan?

Full access to the PMS on up to 3 properties: 8+ OTA channels (Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Expedia, Agoda, Trip.com, Hopper, Google Vacation Rentals and Direct), AI smart pricing, the unified guest inbox, and compliance tracking. No card required and no commitment — it is designed so you can properly try the platform before you scale.

What does Premium cost?

Premium is £9.99 per extra property per month from your 4th property — your first 3 stay free. Airbnb bookings carry 0% commission and other OTAs just 3%. You run the portfolio yourself using the full PMS, with financials and Xero sync, team and cleaner management, and priority support included.

What exactly does the Partner model include?

Becoming a verified Partner is a genuine 50/50 partnership. PropertyFlow takes on every ground operation — linen and laundry, professional cleaning, guest changeovers, logistics and supply, and maintenance — as well as all backend operations: channels, pricing, financials and compliance. You keep guest correspondence and spend your time on outreach, relationships and growing your portfolio.

Can I move between the three models?

Yes. Most operators start on the Free plan, upgrade to Premium as they grow past three properties, and apply to become a Partner when they want to scale hard without being pulled into day-to-day operations. There are no setup fees and no lock-in — change how you work with us whenever you're ready.

Do I need an STL licence, and does PropertyFlow help?

If you let a property in Scotland on a short-term basis (under 31 consecutive days), you need a Short-Term Let licence, and in certain areas you may also need planning permission. PropertyFlow tracks every licence, certificate and deadline on every plan, and Partners have the full compliance process handled as part of the partnership.

Which booking channels are supported?

8+ channels from day one: Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Expedia, Agoda, Trip.com, Hopper, Google Vacation Rentals and Direct — all synced through one calendar so double bookings are designed out. This is included on every plan, including the Free plan.
