Since Scotland's mandatory STL licensing (Oct 2022), compliance has become a full-time job.

Compliance Penalties

STL Licence, Gas Safety (CP12), EICR, EPC — miss one expiry and face prosecution under Scottish law.

Up to £40,000 fines
