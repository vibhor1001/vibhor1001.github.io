Frequently Asked Questions

What is an STL licence and do I need one? ▼

If you let a property in Scotland on a short-term basis (under 31 consecutive days), you need a Short-Term Let licence under the Civic Government (Scotland) Act 1982. In certain areas, you may also need planning permission for secondary letting. PropertyFlow helps you track every licence, certificate, and deadline.

How does PropertyFlow pricing work? ▼

PropertyFlow pricing is one continuous model — no separate plans to choose. Your first 3 properties are always free. From property #4, it's just £9.99 per extra per month. Every OTA channel (Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO, Expedia, Agoda, Trip.com, Hopper, Google Vacation Rentals, Direct) is available from day one — no upgrade required to connect any channel. Airbnb bookings are always 0% commission. Non-Airbnb OTA bookings are 5% per booking while you're on your first 3 free properties, dropping to 3% from your 4th property. No card required to sign up, no setup fees, no lock-in.

How does PropertyFlow work? ▼

PropertyFlow is an all-in-one property management platform that replaces your entire STL tech stack. You get AI-driven pricing, multi-channel distribution across Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO and more, unified guest messaging, compliance tracking, and financial management — all from one dashboard.

What if my local authority requires planning permission? ▼

Planning requirements vary by local authority. PropertyFlow helps you understand your obligations and tracks every stage of the compliance process. Each case is assessed individually based on your property's circumstances and local regulations.

What insurance do I need? ▼

Standard home or buy-to-let policies don't cover STL use. You'll need specialist short-term let insurance for buildings and contents cover. We recommend Pikl, Homeprotect, or Schofields. PropertyFlow's compliance dashboard helps you track insurance expiry dates alongside all other certificates.

Does PropertyFlow include guest messaging tools? ▼

Yes. PropertyFlow's unified inbox brings all OTA guest messages into one screen. Reply to Airbnb, Booking.com, and VRBO guests from one place. Set up message templates, auto-replies for check-in instructions, and never miss a guest inquiry.

How does PropertyFlow handle local tourism levies? ▼

Tourism levies and local taxes vary by region. For example, Edinburgh is introducing a visitor levy from 2026, and other UK cities may follow. PropertyFlow tracks applicable levies for your area and provides tools for collection, reporting, and remittance.
