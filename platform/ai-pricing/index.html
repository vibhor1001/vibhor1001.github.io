Why Cognito Pricing Matters in Edinburgh

Festival Season: Peak Opportunity

During Edinburgh Festival (August), nightly rates typically increase 50–80% (1.5–1.8x base rate). Property owners who miss this window leave thousands on the table. Cognito pricing automatically captures every pound of seasonal demand.

Off-Season: Maintain Occupancy

During quieter months (January, February, November), manually-set prices often sit too high, creating vacancy gaps. Cognito pricing lowers rates intelligently to keep beds filled and revenue flowing.

Manual Pricing Leaves Money on the Table

Set prices too high, and you miss bookings. Set them too low, and you lose revenue. Cognito pricing strikes the perfect balance every single night, responding to market conditions in real time.
