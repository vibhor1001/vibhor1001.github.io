Platform Feature

Compliance Tracking

An automated dashboard that tracks every licence, certificate, and deadline for your property. Nothing lapses. Nothing gets missed. You get alerted before anything expires.

What the Dashboard Tracks

Compliance Area What the Platform Tracks
STL Licence Status Real-time status of temporary and full licence applications. See exactly where your application is in the process.
Planning Applications Track planning application submissions, council responses, and determination dates. All milestones logged.
Appeal Deadlines Statutory appeal windows tracked with countdown timers. Never miss a filing deadline.
Safety Certificates Expiry dates for electrical testing, gas safety, fire safety, and legionella risk assessments. Alerts sent 90 days before renewal.
Insurance Renewals Policy expiry tracking for public liability, employer's liability, and professional indemnity. Renewal reminders automated.
Automated Reminders 90-day, 60-day, and 30-day alerts before any document, licence, or certificate expires. Nothing slips through.

How It Works

1

We Onboard Your Property

We collect all existing licences, certificates, and application details. Everything gets logged into your compliance dashboard.

2

System Tracks All Deadlines

Every expiry date, renewal window, and statutory deadline is tracked automatically. The system knows what's coming before you do.

3

Automated Alerts Before Expiry

You receive alerts at 90, 60, and 30 days before anything expires. We action renewals so nothing ever lapses.

Key Features

90-Day Renewal Reminders

Automated alerts well before any certificate, licence, or policy expires. Enough lead time to renew without rush.

Real-Time Licence Status

See exactly where your STL licence application stands at any moment. Temporary, pending, full — always visible.

Certificate Storage

Deadline Dashboard
