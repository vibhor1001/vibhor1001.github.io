Platform Feature

Manage Your Operations. One Platform.

PropertyFlow gives you the tools to coordinate cleaning teams, track maintenance, manage guest communications, and run your operations from one dashboard.

What's Included in Hands-Free Management

Guest Communication

Unified inbox for all OTA messages. Set up auto-replies for check-in instructions, create message templates, and communicate house rules to every guest from one screen.

Cleaning Team Management

Assign cleaners to properties, auto-create cleaning jobs from bookings, manage checklists with photo verification, and track clock-in/out times. Your cleaning team stays coordinated through the platform.

Linen & Supplies

Fresh linens, premium toiletries, welcome packs (tea, coffee, biscuits), and checkout supplies are stocked before every arrival. Guests feel the care. Reviews reflect it.

Maintenance Tracking

Log maintenance requests, assign tasks to your maintenance team, and track completion. The platform helps you stay on top of property upkeep and coordinate repairs efficiently.

Inspection Checklists

Create custom checklists for post-stay inspections. Your team photographs condition, documents any issues, and logs everything through the platform. Maintain standards across every turnover.

Automated Guest Messaging

Set up automated messages for check-in instructions, house rules, and checkout reminders. Message templates save time. Auto-replies keep guests informed even while you sleep.

Noise Monitoring

Real-time noise detection in common areas. If noise levels breach your quiet hours, our team receives an alert and can intervene before neighbours complain. Keeps your compliance record clean.

Property Preparation

PropertyFlow helps you prepare your property for guests -- premium staging guidance, professional photography coordination, interior design consultation, and listing optimisation. Your property, elevated.
