Our dedicated Edinburgh teams handle everything from professional cleaning to emergency maintenance. You own the asset. We manage the operation.

What's Included in Hands-Free Management

Guest Vetting

Rigorous screening process for every booking. We verify identity, check reviews across platforms, communicate house rules, and flag high-risk bookings before they arrive. Your property stays safe.

Professional Cleaning

Hotel-standard deep clean between every guest. Our teams use hospital-grade disinfectants, check every corner, and document the clean with photos. Turnaround time: 3–4 hours for full turnover.

Linen & Supplies

Fresh linens, premium toiletries, welcome packs (tea, coffee, biscuits), and checkout supplies are stocked before every arrival. Guests feel the care. Reviews reflect it.

Maintenance

Same-day response for urgent repairs across Edinburgh. Leaky taps, heating issues, Wi-Fi problems — our maintenance team handles it. Preventative inspections catch issues before they become emergencies.

Regular Inspections

Property checked between every stay. We photograph condition, document any damage, verify all amenities are working, and identify maintenance needs proactively. Your property asset stays protected.

24/7 Guest Support

Round-the-clock WhatsApp and phone support for guests. Check-in help, amenity questions, lost keys, emergency services coordination — we handle it so you don't have to wake up at 2am.

Noise Monitoring

Real-time noise detection in common areas. If noise levels breach your quiet hours, our team receives an alert and can intervene before neighbours complain. Keeps your compliance record clean.

Property Preparation (Corporate Let)

For properties marketed as corporate lets, we offer premium staging, professional photography, interior design consultation, and decor refresh. Transform a standard property into a premium corporate asset.

Why Hands-Free Management Matters

Time is Money

Edinburgh landlords spend 15–20 hours per week managing a single property: scheduling cleaners, responding to guest messages, arranging repairs, dealing with complaints. Hands-free management frees up your time for your real business.

Professional Operations Beat Amateur

Guests notice when a property is professionally managed: quick responses, pristine condition, attentive service. Professional properties earn 15–25% higher reviews and 30% higher booking rates than self-managed properties.
