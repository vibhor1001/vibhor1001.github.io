2. Data We Collect

We collect personal data through the following channels:

2.1 Information You Provide via Website Forms

When you submit a form on our website (e.g., the contact form, property audit request, or early adopter sign-up), we collect:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property details (address, type, location)
  • "What best describes you" selection (landlord, investor, developer, agent, or other)
  • Number of properties
  • Any additional information you provide in the message field

2.2 Information Collected Automatically via Cookies and Third-Party Services

When you visit our website, certain data is collected automatically through cookies and third-party services embedded on our pages:

  • HubSpot tracking cookies -- page views, session duration, referral source, form interactions, and return visits

  • Tailwind CSS CDN -- your IP address may be transmitted to Cloudflare when the stylesheet is loaded

2.3 Information Stored in Our CRM (HubSpot)

If you engage with PropertyFlow as a lead, enquiry, or client, we store the following data within our CRM system (HubSpot, EU1 region):

  • Contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Deal pipeline data (enquiry stage, property details, service type, contract status)
  • Email engagement data (opens, clicks, replies to emails sent via HubSpot)
  • Notes from calls or meetings
  • Associated property and service records

6. Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. We use the following cookies:

Cookie / Service Provider Purpose Duration Type
__hstc HubSpot Tracks visitor identity. Contains domain, utk, initial timestamp, last timestamp, current timestamp, and session number 13 months Analytics
hubspotutk HubSpot Keeps track of a visitor's identity. Passed to HubSpot on form submission to de-duplicate contacts 13 months Analytics
__hssc HubSpot Keeps track of sessions. Used to determine if HubSpot should increment the session number and timestamps in the __hstc cookie 30 minutes Analytics
__hssrc HubSpot Determines if the visitor has restarted their browser. If this cookie does not exist when HubSpot manages cookies, it is considered a new session Session Analytics
messagesUtk HubSpot Used to recognise visitors who chat via the messages tool, if enabled in future 13 months Functional
Google Fonts CSS Google Font delivery. No persistent cookies are set, but your IP address is transmitted to Google servers to load the Inter font files N/A Necessary
Tailwind CSS CDN Cloudflare CSS framework delivery. No persistent cookies are set, but your IP address is transmitted to Cloudflare servers to load the stylesheet N/A Necessary

No Advertising Cookies

PropertyFlow does not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or any third-party advertising tracking technology. We do not serve ads on our website and we do not sell or share your browsing data with advertising networks.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to submit forms.

8. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred outside the United Kingdom in the following circumstances:

HubSpot (EU1 Region)

Your CRM data is hosted on HubSpot's EU1 infrastructure within the European Union. The EU has been granted an adequacy decision by the UK Government under the UK GDPR, meaning EU data protection standards are considered equivalent to UK standards. No additional safeguards are required for this transfer.

Google Fonts (United States)

When loading web fonts, your IP address is transmitted to Google servers, which may be located in the United States. This transfer is protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the UK Information Commissioner's Office, and Google's participation in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

Cloudflare (Global CDN)

Cloudflare operates a global content delivery network. When loading the Tailwind CSS framework, your request may be served from the nearest Cloudflare edge server, which could be located outside the UK. Cloudflare maintains Standard Contractual Clauses and has been certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

GitHub Pages (United States)

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. Server access logs (IP address, user-agent) may be processed by GitHub (Microsoft) in the United States. This transfer is protected by Standard Contractual Clauses and Microsoft's participation in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

9. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will respond within one calendar month of receiving your request.

Right to Rectification

You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data where there is no compelling reason for its continued processing. This is also known as the "right to be forgotten."

Right to Restriction

You have the right to request that we restrict processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, for example while we verify the accuracy of data you have challenged.

Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format, and to transmit that data to another controller.

Right to Object

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interest or direct marketing. If you object, we will stop processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

How to Exercise Your Rights

There is no fee to exercise your rights. However, we may charge a reasonable fee if your request is clearly unfounded, repetitive, or excessive.

10. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • ● Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • ● Secure CRM: HubSpot (our CRM provider) maintains SOC 2 Type II certification, ISO 27001 compliance, and encrypts data at rest and in transit
  • ● Access controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorised PropertyFlow staff on a need-to-know basis
  • ● Regular review: We regularly review our data processing activities and security measures to ensure ongoing compliance

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security breach affecting your data, please contact us immediately.
